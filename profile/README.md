## Welcome to Exein 

![Fleet of Exein devices](/images/github-cover-4.2.png)

**Exein is building a healthier future for digital life by protecting your Physical AI Systems with self-sustaining cybersecurity solutions.**

### Ecosystem

There are many ways Exein can be used to make your devices more secure. Here are some to get you started:

- [Pulsar](https://github.com/Exein-io/pulsar) - A modular and blazing fast runtime security tool for **Physical AI Systems**, powered by eBPF.
- [Kepler](https://github.com/Exein-io/kepler) - Open Source **CVE search engine** written entirely in Rust.
